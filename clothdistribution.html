Distribution Drive

Nyasa, in collaboration with the institute's social outreach, distributed essential items during the pandemic, such as masks, reusable sanitary napkins, mosquito repellent coils, fruit packets, and sweets. The volunteers also conducted a ‘sweets and fruits distribution’ drive at the construction workers' colony. Nyasa also paid gratitude to the entire contractual and housekeeping staff for their endless effort in helping the community by continuously providing essential services during the lockdown period.
